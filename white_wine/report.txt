**Business Recommendations & Value **
This model provides actionable insights that can directly impact production consistency and profitability.
Implement as a Digital QC Tool:
Since the model excels at identifying "Good" wine (84% recall), it can be used on the production line. By feeding the chemical properties of a new batch into the model, you can get an instant prediction of its quality. This allows you to flag batches that may not meet the "Good" standard before bottling, saving time and resources.
Optimize Production Levers:
To actively improve wine quality and move more products into the "Good" and "Best" categories, the data strongly suggests the production team should focus on:
Maximizing: alcohol content.
Minimizing: density and volatile acidity.
Improve "Bad" & "Best" Detection:
To make the model effective at spotting high-profit "Best" wines or cost-saving "Bad" wines, we must gather more data on these categories. I recommend implementing a system to tag and record the chemical data for batches identified (by human tasters) as "Best" or "Bad." This will allow us to retrain and dramatically improve the model's accuracy on these valuable categories.
